Question: How to boot it up?
Answer: Press the power button at the right for
more than 3 seconds.

Question: While scanning the two-dimensional
code,I received the prompt that the wristwatch
had been powered off?
Answer: Please confirm if you have booted it up
or not.Alternatively you can boot it up twice,
and wait for 30 to 60 seconds during every
attempt before doing the scanning.If problems
still remain, Please confirm whether the SIM

card is normal or arrears, or call the customer service.

 

  Question: Why does it take 2 minutes or even
longer to receive the chatting message?
Answer: To save power, the system will receive
chatting messages every 2 or 10 minutes by
default. If you do have an urgent need
to contact the wristwatch, you can directly
dial the wristwatch number.

Question: I have dialed the wristwatch number,
but therewas no response?
Answer: Please confirm whether or not the
wristwatch has been powered on or if the
disturbing-free function has been enabled
or not.

Question: I click the wristwatch positioning in
the map, but the location seems to be inaccurate?
Answer: By default, GPS positioning is used by
the system, and the click of more than 5 counts
daily is not allowed. After each click, please
wait for several minutes before you view the
location of the wristwatch. In case the GPS
positioning fails, location-based-service will
be automatically enabled (by default the
location will deviate several hundred meters).

Question: Who is the map supplier?
Answer: The map is supplied by AutoNavi.

Question: Is the service still available abroad?
Answer: Only if your wristwatch SIM card
supports roaming, the service is still available
abroad.

Question: Will the wristwatch cause radiation?
Answer: Conforming to EU CE Standards, the
wristwatch causes as much radiation as a
small cell phone. Please rest assured!

Question: Is the wristwatch waterproof?
Answer: Having passed the waterproof rating
test, the wristwatch can be worn in domestic
water related circumstances, for example
washing hands.

Question: Will skins get allergic to the
wristwatch and watchband?
Answer: Made from food silicone, the
wristwatch won't cause any side effect. Please
rest assured.

Question: How to view the wristwatch's electric
quantity?
Answer: Battery indicator has been built in the
wristwatch. If there is only 1 unit of electric
quantity left, please charge it as soon as
possible.

Question: How long can the wristwatch work
after each charging?
Answer: It can work 2 to 4 days if fully charged.
